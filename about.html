About

A house built on patience
and good books.

Limouze Books is an independent publishing house. We exist because we believe the world is better off with more carefully made books in it — books written by authors who have something to say, and edited with the time and attention they deserve.

We don’t publish quickly. We publish well.

Our list is intentionally small. Every title we put into the world is one we have read closely, believed in fully, and shaped together with its author. We are not interested in trends or quotas. We are interested in stories that last — and in the readers, young and old, who will find themselves in them.

The house is young. Our first author is Daisy Harper Gray-Lane, whose Dream Big, She Did series brings to life the women who shaped our world — told the way they always should have been. More authors will join in the seasons to come.

If you are a reader, welcome. If you are an author whose work might find a home with us, we would be glad to hear from you.

What we believe

Three things we hold to

One

Slow is a feature

A book takes the time it takes. We do not rush authors, manuscripts, or readers.

Two

Authors first

The voice on the page is the author’s. Our job is to protect it, sharpen it, and put it in front of the right readers.

Three

Made with care

From the first edit to the last detail of the printed page, every book is held to the same simple test: would we be proud to give it as a gift?
