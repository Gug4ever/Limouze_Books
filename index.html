The House

A small house with a long view

Limouze Books was founded on a simple idea: the best books are the ones that take their time. We are an independent publisher with no rush to grow, and no interest in noise — only in the quiet, careful work of putting good stories into the hands of readers.

Every book we publish is a book we believe in.

We work closely with each author, from the first manuscript to the last detail of the printed page. Our list will always be small — and that, we think, is the point.
